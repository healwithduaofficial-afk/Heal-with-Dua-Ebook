Chapter Five
Hope & Optimism
When dawn breaks after the longest night

Introduction

Hope in Islam is not naive positivity — it is active trust. It is planting seeds while trusting the rain will come. It is the Prophet ﷺ leaving Makkah after years of persecution, knowing Allah would open Makkah's hearts. It is the migrant leaving everything behind, believing Allah will replace with better.

This chapter is for the moments when you need to remember: Allah's mercy is bigger than your mistakes, and His plan is wider than your vision.

The Dua for Ease in Affairs

اللَّهُمَّ لَا سَهْلَ إِلَّا مَا جَعَلْتَهُ سَهْلًا، وَأَنْتَ تَجْعَلُ الْحَزْنَ إِذَا شِئْتَ سَهْلًا
Allahumma la sahla illa ma ja'altahu sahlan, wa Anta taj'alul-hazna idha shi'ta sahlan.
"O Allah, there is no ease except in that which You have made easy, and You make the difficulty, if You wish, easy."
Reference: Ibn Hibban

The Dua of Prophet Musa (AS)

رَبِّ إِنِّي لِمَا أَنْزَلْتَ إِلَيَّ مِنْ خَيْرٍ فَقِيرٌ
Rabbi inni lima anzalta ilayya min khayrin faqir.
"My Lord, indeed I am, for whatever good You would send down to me, in need."
Reference: Surah Al-Qasas 28:24

Origin Story

Prophet Musa (AS) fled Egypt after accidentally killing a man. He arrived in Madyan exhausted, thirsty, and alone — a fugitive with nothing. He helped two women water their sheep at a well, then sat under a tree in utter desperation. He didn't ask for wealth, power, or revenge. He simply said: "I am in need of whatever good You send down."

Allah answered with a home, a family, and a mission that would change history. This dua teaches us that admitting our poverty before Allah is the first step to receiving His abundance.

The first dua reminds us that ease is not found in circumstances — it is found in Allah's decree. What seems impossible to us is simple for Him.

And whoever fears Allah — He will make for him a way out, and will provide for him from where he does not expect. And whoever relies upon Allah — then He is sufficient for him. Surah At-Talaq 65:2-3

  3. What would I attempt today if I truly believed Allah would provide?
